## PROMPT OVERVIEW
Transform any story into a mystical, cottagecore narrative that captures the introspective magic of Taylor Swift's Folklore and Evermore eras. Perfect for creating authentic, emotionally-rich tales that resonate with teens seeking deeper meaning and connection to nature.

## CORE AESTHETIC & THEMES
- **Visual Palette**: Muted earth tones, golden hour lighting, vintage film grain, handwritten fonts
- **Emotional Landscape**: Nostalgic melancholy, quiet introspection, bittersweet growth, found family bonds
- **Setting Elements**: Small towns, misty forests, cozy cabins, handwritten letters, vintage cardigans, autumn leaves

## STORY TRANSFORMATION INSTRUCTIONS

### CHARACTER ARCHETYPES (Choose 1-2):
- **The Dreamer**: Introverted teen who finds solace in nature journaling and vintage photography
- **The Wise Friend**: That one person everyone turns to for advice, carries emotional weight of others
- **The Lost Soul**: Recently moved to a small town, seeking belonging and authentic connections
- **The Creative**: Writes poetry, plays acoustic guitar, sees magic in everyday moments
- **The Healer**: Naturally empathetic, helps others through difficult times with gentle wisdom

### NARRATIVE ELEMENTS TO WEAVE IN:
1. **Mental Health Awareness**: Subtle discussions of anxiety, depression, healing through nature
2. **Intergenerational Wisdom**: Elderly characters sharing life lessons through stories
3. **Environmental Connection**: Characters finding peace and answers in natural settings
4. **Digital Detox Themes**: Choosing real connections over social media validation
5. **Found Family**: Creating chosen family bonds that heal past wounds

### STORYTELLING STRUCTURE:
- **Opening**: Character in contemplative moment in nature setting
- **Conflict**: Internal struggle with identity, belonging, or emotional healing
- **Journey**: Slow discovery through meaningful conversations and quiet moments
- **Resolution**: Gentle growth and acceptance, not dramatic transformation
- **Ending**: Hopeful but realistic, emphasizing ongoing journey rather than "fixed" problems

### GENZ-SPECIFIC ELEMENTS:
- Authentic emotional vocabulary (no toxic positivity)
- Diverse characters with various backgrounds and orientations
- Climate consciousness woven naturally into plot
- Social justice themes through character actions, not preaching
- Technology used mindfully, not avoided entirely

### INTERACTIVE ENGAGEMENT FEATURES:
- Include seasonal aesthetic details readers can recreate (cozy sweater descriptions, autumn recipes)
- Embed discussion questions about mental health and self-discovery
- Create "aesthetic mood boards" through detailed sensory descriptions
- Include playlist suggestions matching story moments
- Add journaling prompts inspired by character growth

### LANGUAGE & TONE:
Use introspective, poetic language with:
- Metaphors connecting emotions to nature
- Sensory details emphasizing touch, sound, scent
- Internal monologue revealing character depth
- Dialogue that feels authentic to teen speech patterns
- Pacing that allows for reflection between scenes

### SOCIAL MEDIA OPTIMIZATION:
- Create quotable moments perfect for Instagram captions
- Include aesthetic details that inspire photo recreations
- Develop shareable character quotes about growth and healing
- Structure scenes that work as individual TikTok story moments
- End chapters with thought-provoking questions for comments

## EXAMPLE STORY SEEDS:
1. A teen moves to their grandmother's small town and discovers a box of love letters that reveal family secrets about choosing authentic love over societal expectations.

2. Two former best friends reconnect during a power outage that forces their whole town to slow down and rediscover what really matters.

3. A group of teens starts a community garden to help their struggling town, learning about nurturing growth in plants and themselves.
