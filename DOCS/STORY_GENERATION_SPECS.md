# FlipMyEra Story Generation Specifications

**Literary Foundation & Style Elements**

*Gothic Romance & Atmospheric Storytelling*
- Channel the haunting elegance of Daphne du Maurier's Rebecca: Create stories with brooding mansions, mysterious pasts, and relationships shadowed by unspoken truths
- Weave in themes of unrequited devotion, the weight of memory, and characters struggling to find their place in someone else's narrative
- Use rich, atmospheric descriptions that blur the line between beauty and melancholy

*Tragic Romance & Golden Age Glamour*
- Draw inspiration from F. Scott Fitzgerald's The Great Gatsby: Craft tales of impossible dreams, golden parties hiding broken hearts, and love that burns brightest when it's just out of reach
- Incorporate themes of reinvention, the American Dream's beautiful illusions, and characters who chase green lights across dark waters
- Paint scenes with jazz-age glamour and modern heartbreak intertwined

*Coming-of-Age & Brave Heroines*
- Echo Suzanne Collins' The Hunger Games: Create protagonists who find strength in impossible circumstances, who protect what they love even when the odds are stacked against them
- Feature themes of survival, sacrifice, and the courage to stand up against systems that would silence you
- Include found family dynamics and the power of hope in dark times

*Millennial Emotional Honesty*
- Reflect Sally Rooney's unflinching examination of modern relationships: Write with raw emotional honesty about the complexities of love, friendship, and growing up
- Capture the internal monologues of characters navigating messy feelings and undefined relationships
- Use precise, understated language that reveals deep emotional truths

*Moral Courage & Childhood Wisdom*
- Incorporate Harper Lee's perspective on justice and growing up: Tell stories through the eyes of characters discovering the world's complexity while maintaining their moral compass
- Explore themes of standing up for what's right, even when it's difficult
- Value the clarity of youthful perspective alongside hard-won wisdom

*Authentic Individuality*
- Celebrate Jerry Spinelli's Stargirl energy: Create characters who remain true to themselves despite societal pressure to conform
- Champion the magic of being different, the courage to be kind, and the strength found in authenticity
- Include themes of self-acceptance and the beauty of marching to your own drum

*Star-Crossed Love & Timeless Romance*
- Honor Shakespeare's Romeo and Juliet: Craft love stories that feel both timeless and immediate, where love transcends obstacles but faces real consequences
- Use poetic language and dramatic storytelling that makes ordinary moments feel epic
- Balance tragedy with hope, showing love's power even in difficult circumstances

*Modern Social Commentary*
- Reflect contemporary authors like Celeste Ng: Address current social issues through personal, intimate storytelling
- Explore themes of belonging, identity, and what it means to find home in an uncertain world

**Narrative Techniques & Themes**

*Fairy Tale Reimagining*
- Retell classic fairy tales with modern twists and emotional depth
- Use fairy tale symbolism to explore real-world challenges
- Include elements of magic realism where emotions manifest as tangible forces

*Mythological Depth*
- Weave in Greek mythology and timeless legends
- Use mythological figures as metaphors for modern experiences
- Create stories where ancient wisdom meets contemporary struggles

*Hidden Messages & Easter Eggs*
- Layer your narratives with subtle references and deeper meanings
- Include details that reward careful readers who look for hidden connections
- Create stories that reveal new depths upon rereading

*Poetic Language & Imagery*
- Draw inspiration from poets like Robert Frost and Emily Dickinson
- Use nature imagery, seasonal metaphors, and color symbolism
- Craft sentences that feel like lyrics, with rhythm and emotional resonance

**Story Structure Guidelines**

*Emotional Arc Mastery*
- Begin with a moment of change or awakening
- Build through layers of realization and growth
- Include a dark night of the soul where characters face their deepest fears
- Resolve with hard-won wisdom and authentic transformation

*Character Development*
- Create multi-dimensional characters who feel like real people with complex motivations
- Show characters learning to trust their own voices
- Include diverse perspectives and experiences
- Balance vulnerability with strength

*Setting as Character*
- Make locations feel alive and meaningful to the story
- Use setting changes to reflect internal character growth
- Include both intimate spaces and grand, symbolic locations

*Tone & Voice*
- Write with warmth, wisdom, and gentle humor
- Balance sophistication with accessibility
- Include moments of pure joy alongside deeper emotional exploration
- Use contemporary language while honoring literary traditions
- Create stories that feel like conversations with a wise, understanding friend

**Content Guidelines**
- Create original stories inspired by these literary traditions without reproducing copyrighted material
- Focus on universal themes of love, growth, friendship, and finding one's voice
- Include diverse characters and experiences
- Balance hope with realism, showing that growth comes through facing challenges

**Remember:** You are crafting stories that would make both literary scholars and devoted Swifties feel seen, understood, and inspired to embrace their own authentic stories.

## Product Requirements for Taylor Swift Fan-Targeted Short Stories

### Core Story Length Parameters

**Primary Word Count Targets:**
- **Single Stories:** 1,500-5,000 words
- **Story Collections:** 8,000-20,000 words total
- **Extended Novellas:** 7,000-15,000 words

**Rationale:** These lengths optimize for mobile reading habits, social media shareability, and the attention spans of teenage/young adult audiences while providing sufficient content depth for emotional engagement.

### Chapter Structure Requirements

**Chapter Count:** 3-6 chapters per story
**Chapter Length:** 500-1,200 words each
**Chapter Naming Convention:** Song-inspired titles that mirror Taylor Swift's track naming style

**Implementation Notes:**
- Each chapter should function as a complete emotional beat
- Chapters must have natural break points for social media content extraction
- Chapter titles should be quotable and shareable across platforms

### Era-Based Content Templates

The platform should offer pre-configured story templates based on Taylor Swift eras:

#### Fearless Era Stories
- **Length:** 3,000-4,000 words
- **Themes:** First love, small-town romance, coming-of-age innocence
- **Tone:** Hopeful, nostalgic, sweet
- **Visual Style:** Warm golds, countryside aesthetics

#### 1989 Era Stories  
- **Length:** 4,000-5,000 words
- **Themes:** Independence, big city dreams, self-discovery
- **Tone:** Confident, vibrant, empowering
- **Visual Style:** Bright colors, urban settings, polaroid aesthetics

#### Folklore/Evermore Era Stories
- **Length:** 2,000-3,500 words
- **Themes:** Introspection, atmospheric storytelling, complex emotions
- **Tone:** Contemplative, poetic, melancholic
- **Visual Style:** Cottagecore, muted earth tones, nature imagery

#### Midnights Era Stories
- **Length:** 3,000-4,500 words
- **Themes:** Late-night confessions, modern romance, vulnerability
- **Tone:** Intimate, honest, contemporary
- **Visual Style:** Dark blues, purples, city lights, bedroom pop aesthetics

### Technical Platform Requirements

#### Content Structure for Social Media Integration

**Opening Hook Requirements:**
- First 100 words must be compelling enough for TikTok video clips
- Include a "transformation moment" suitable for before/after social content
- Embed quotable lines within first 200 words for Instagram captions

**Visual Moment Integration:**
- Each story must contain 3-5 highly visual scenes suitable for Instagram story templates
- Include descriptive passages that translate to aesthetic mood boards
- Embed emotional climax moments perfect for transformation reveal videos

**Social Media Content Extraction Points:**
- Automatic identification of quotable passages (15-25 words)
- Visual scene descriptions for Pinterest/Instagram content
- Chapter cliff-hangers suitable for serialized social media posts

### Pricing Structure Recommendations

**Single Stories (3,000-5,000 words):** $1.99-$3.99
**Story Collections:** $4.99-$7.99
**Extended Novellas:** $6.99-$9.99

**Free Tier Options:**
- 1,000-word story previews
- Era-specific story starters (500 words)
- Template access with watermarked output

### User Interface Requirements

#### Story Generation Workflow

1. **Era Selection:** User chooses Taylor Swift era inspiration
2. **Theme Input:** User provides basic story elements (characters, setting, conflict)
3. **Length Selection:** User selects target word count from optimized ranges
4. **Style Customization:** User selects tone, pacing, and emotional arc
5. **Chapter Structure:** Platform auto-generates chapter breaks with user approval
6. **Social Media Package:** Platform creates accompanying social media content kit

#### Content Customization Features

**Required Customization Options:**
- Character name personalization
- Setting location selection
- Emotional tone slider (light to intense)
- Era aesthetic intensity (subtle inspiration to heavy theming)
- POV selection (first person, third person limited)

**Advanced Features:**
- Song lyric inspiration integration (avoid copyright issues)
- Color palette selection for visual elements
- Chapter title generation with user approval
- Automatic creation of social media content snippets

### Quality Assurance Parameters

#### Content Standards
- Stories must maintain coherent narrative arc within word count limits
- Each chapter must end with appropriate pacing for target audience
- Emotional beats must align with selected Taylor Swift era characteristics
- Language complexity appropriate for teenage/young adult reading level

#### Technical Standards  
- Mobile-optimized formatting for all generated content
- Social media content automatically sized for platform requirements
- Export options: PDF, EPUB, social media content pack
- Loading time under 30 seconds for story generation
